Thesis

Africa's digital strategy was built on three pillars: outsourced digital jobs as a development ladder, cheap smartphones as the access gateway, and leapfrogging into the global digital economy. Frontier AI is pressuring all three at once — automating the jobs ladder, pricing out the device gateway, and concentrating compute away from the continent.
